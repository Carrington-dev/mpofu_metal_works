Services

We offer a wide range of metal and steel related services some of which are given below

Fabrication

We manufacture structural steel for the industrial and commercials sectors ranging from Warehousing, Retail shopping centres, car dealerships to structural steel pedestrian bridges.

Learn more

Plasma Cutting

We currently process over 20 tons of plate a month through this plasma cutting facility. Please Contact Us should you wish to enquire about plasma cutting

Learn more

Cutting services

We offer a wide variety of cutting services including cut-to-length, laser cutting, water jet cutting, plasma cutting, shearing, slitting, sawing and band saw cutting.

Learn more

Polishing and brushing services

We have many options for polishing and brushing services including basic polishing and brushing, coil-to-coil as well as sheet-to-sheet polishing and brushing.

Learn more

Welding services

For welding services we offer mechanized welding, submerged arc welding (saw) and plasma arc welding (paw) for beams and hollow sections.

Learn more

Plate bending and other services

We also offer other services which are blanking, multi-blanking, traverse winding, plate bending and milling/edge preparation.

Learn more
